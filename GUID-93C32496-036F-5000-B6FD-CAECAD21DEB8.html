certtool

certtool is a eshell program for inserting certificates and manipulating their settings.

  • certtool Overview
    The Symbian platform provides a certificate store, certstore for short, which is a repository of root and user certificates which can be retrieved and used to accomplish a variety of tasks. The certstore is initially empty. certtool is a a user-friendly eshell program for inserting certificates and manipulating their settings.
