Introduction to Mapping between Symbian UIDs and vCard Properties

Symbian uses unique IDs to identify vCard properties and property parameters. The mapping between vCard properties and Symbian-specific constants is done by the cntvcard component cntvcard.dll (see UIDs that Identify vCard Properties).
