Generic Services

Generic Services is a collection of unrelated components that provide generic services to other components, applications and libraries of the Symbian platform. The services include support for active backup, handling Uniform Resource Indicators (URIs), recognising Multipurpose Internet Mail Extension (MIME) types and scheduling tasks.
