RMobileLocationServices::THorzVelocityV1 Class Reference

class RMobileLocationServices::THorzVelocityV1

Horizontal velocity is characterised by the horizontal speed and bearing. The horizontal speed gives the magnitude of the horizontal component of the velocity of a UE. The bearing provides the direction of the horizontal component of velocity taken clockwise from North. 3GPP TS 23.032
