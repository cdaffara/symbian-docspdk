TPhonebookStoreInfoV1()

TPhonebookStoreInfoV1()[inline]

This structure contains the details required by the CTSY about the various phonebook stores, it is used in the initialisation procedure.

Total Entries - The total number of entries the store can contain. Maximum Text Length - The maximum length of a text field in the store. Maximum Number Length - The maximum length of a number in the store. Capabilities - The Bit field of the stores capabilities (RMobilePhoneStore::TMobilePhoneStoreCaps). Maximum Fields Per Entry - The maximum number of fields in a given entry.
