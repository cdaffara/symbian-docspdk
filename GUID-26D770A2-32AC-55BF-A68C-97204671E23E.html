The following classes are available as a part of the SIP NAT Traversal Controller API:

CSIPNATTraversalController, TSIPNATTraversalControllerInitParams, MSIPNATTraversalRequestObserver, MSIPNATBindingObserver.

The KSIPNATTraversalControllerIFUid is a constant that is available as a part of the SIP NAT Traversal Controller API.
