Browsing and downloading

The term browsing refers to the ability to browse, for example, Web or WAP pages in order to access content. Browser application services allow you to create plug-ins for the browser that are registered and available when the browser is open. In addition, you can open the browser inside your application and handle bookmarks.

The term downloading refers to the ability of the mobile device users to download mobile content, such as ring tones, wallpapers, or games to devices from Web sites, for example.

The term is also related to the concept of digital rights management (DRM) and reliable download technologies.
