Portuguese National Language Support

Portuguese plug-in converters support the Portuguese SMS encoding using the National Language Encoding.

This section describes the different Portuguese plug-in converters supported by the Symbian Platform and the alphabets supported by the individual converters.

Introduction

The Symbian platform provides three plug-in converters to support Portuguese SMS encoding.

  • Default GSM 7bit & Portuguese Single Shift Converter: This converter is identified by KCharacterSetIdentifierPortugueseSingleSms7Bit UID.

  • Portuguese Locking Shift & GSM 7Bit Extension Converter: This converter is identified by the KCharacterSetIdentifierPortugueseLockingSms7Bit UID.

  • Portuguese Locking Shift & Single Shift Converter: This converter is identified by the KCharacterSetIdentifierPortugueseLockingAndSingleSms7Bit UID.

The UID's are defined in the charconv.h file.

Note: For all the converters, any undefined Unicode character is converted to the GSM replacement character (?)0x3F and undefined GSM character is converted to the Unicode replacement character 0xFFFD.
