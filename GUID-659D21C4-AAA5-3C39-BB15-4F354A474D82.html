RMobileLocationServices::THorzWithVertVelocityV1 Class Reference

class RMobileLocationServices::THorzWithVertVelocityV1

Horizontal velocity is characterised by the horizontal speed and bearing. The horizontal speed gives the magnitude of the horizontal component of the velocity of a UE. The vertical speed and direction provides the component of velocity of a UE in a vertical direction. The bearing provides the direction of the horizontal component of velocity taken clockwise from North. 3GPP TS 23.032
