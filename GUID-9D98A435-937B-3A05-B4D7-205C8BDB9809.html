TBool iAddlAssistanceDataReq

TBool iAddlAssistanceDataReq

ETrue indicates that the UE is requested to send the IE "Additional assistance Data Request" when the IE "UE positioning Error" is present in the UE positioning measured results. EFalse indicates that the UE shall use the assistance data available.
