class RSat::TLocalConnectionEventV2 : public RSat::TEventDownloadBaseV2

To inform the UICC that a "Local Connection Event" occurred, call RSat::EventDownload(), passing it the new connection information in a RSat::TLocalConnectionEventV2Pckg, a packaged RSat::TLocalConnectionEventV2. Fill in the address of the new device.
